## FAQ

**Q: What counts as an "application"?**
A: Each distinct product you distribute. Multiple builds of the same application (Windows, macOS, Linux) count as one application.

**Q: How is annual revenue calculated?**
A: Total gross revenue for your organization in the most recent fiscal year.

**Q: Can I evaluate Hermes before purchasing?**
A: Yes. The Elastic License 2.0 permits full use for evaluation. You only need a paid license when shipping a commercial product above the revenue threshold.

**Q: What if my organization grows past $1M revenue?**
A: You'll need to purchase a license before your next release after crossing the threshold.
